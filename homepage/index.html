Why we built this!

Because loosing something is stressfull

Finding it shouldn't be.

Every day, students and staff lose important belongings such as phones, wallets, ID cards, bags, keys, and other personal items. Many of these valuables are never returned—not because no one finds them, but because there is no simple and centralized way to report or locate them.


Our

Lost & Found Portal

was created to bridge this gap. We provide a trusted platform where anyone can quickly report a lost or found item, search existing listings, and securely connect with the rightful owner. Our goal is to make the recovery process fast, transparent, and stress-free for everyone on campus.
